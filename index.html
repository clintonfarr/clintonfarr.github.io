Dallas-Fort Worth House Prices and Market Trends

Visualize the change in median house prices with a map view of the DFW metroplex from 2012 - 2021.

January 15, 2022


Median house prices in the DFW metroplex are delineated by zip code in this map focused Tableau dashboard. Housing data spans from 2012 to 2021. The % change in median house price (YoY) can be viewed by pressing the blue buttom in the bottom left hand corner of the dashboard.

Notable skills used in this project: postgreSQL (joins, lag and window functions), Tableau (map views, dynamic filters).

Personal Finance Dashboard

A categorical time series of net worth visualizing the magnitude of various assets and liabilities.

December 28, 2021

A relational database consisting of nine financial accounts (e.g. checking accounts, savings, loans) was created and queried using pgAdmin4 to create a Tableau dashboard that tracks income, spending, and net worth. Some metrics and axes in the dashboard are hidden for general privacy.

Notable skills used in this project: postgreSQL (joins, views, window function, intervals)
