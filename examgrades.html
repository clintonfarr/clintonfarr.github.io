Exam Grades Dashboard

Analyze the grades of 85 students emphasizing 3 major exams in a dynamic dashboard.

January 9, 2022



Background:
Standardized exam scores are utilized by educators as a metric of the academic progress of students. As an educator, I desired a dynamic dashboard capable of quickly visualizing the exam grades of students across numerous class periods while also incorporating other valuable educational indicators.

Process:
Three distinct exams are incorporated into the dashboard. The data in each plot adapts to the user selection of each exam. This feature could not be accomplished by filtering and required the combination of a parameter and calculated field.



Multiple data tables were converted to a relational database in pgAdmin4. This necessitated the use of several joins in the final export query. This query can be viewed below.
